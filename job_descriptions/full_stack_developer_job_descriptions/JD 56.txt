Job Title: Junior Full Stack Developer
Company: ConsultBae Jobs

About the job
Overview

The Junior Full Stack Developer plays a crucial role in our organization, contributing to the development, implementation, and maintenance of our web applications. They will work closely with senior developers to create efficient and user-friendly interfaces, troubleshoot and resolve software issues, and participate in the full software development lifecycle.

Key Responsibilities

Participate in the design and implementation of front-end and back-end solutions
Develop and maintain web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Write and optimize SQL queries to manipulate data
Collaborate with the design team to improve user experience
Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs
Assist in troubleshooting and resolving software issues
Contribute to code reviews and documentation
Stay updated on emerging technologies and best practices
Work on both client and server-side development
Deploy and maintain applications on cloud services
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features
Follow agile methodologies and participate in sprint planning and estimation
Test and debug software applications
Assist in the evaluation and implementation of new technologies
Provide technical support and training to internal teams

Required Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
1-2 years of experience in web development
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Working knowledge of Node.js and React
Experience with version control systems such as Git
Strong understanding of database management and SQL
Ability to troubleshoot and debug issues in a timely manner
Familiarity with agile development methodologies
Solid understanding of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
Excellent problem-solving and analytical abilities
Ability to work independently and in a team environment
Good communication and collaboration skills
Understanding of software development lifecycle and best practices
Proactive attitude towards learning and self-improvement
Experience with cloud services and deployment is a plus

Skills: html,javascript,node.js,react,sql,git
