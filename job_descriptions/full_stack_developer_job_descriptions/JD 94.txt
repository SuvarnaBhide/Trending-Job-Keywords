Job Title: Full Stack Developer
Company: Findoc

Job Description
Findoc is hiring for the role of Full Stack Developer!

Responsibilities of the Candidate:

Designing, developing, and deploying web-based applications, APIs, and microservices for stock market analysis, trading, and portfolio management.
Developing front-end interfaces to deliver intuitive and responsive user experiences.
Developing back-end services and systems using languages such as Python and frameworks like Django.
Integrating third-party APIs, market data feeds, and financial databases to retrieve, process, and visualize real-time and historical market data.
Creating databases and servers: These systems must be resilient to outages and must function endlessly.
Ensuring Cross Platform compatibility: Applications must run smoothly on all operating systems like Windows, MacOS, and Linux.
Implementing security measures and best practices to ensure the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of sensitive financial information.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)
Proven experience of 2-4 years as a Full Stack Developer, preferably in the financial services or stock market domain
Proficiency in front-end development technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks/libraries
Strong understanding of back-end development concepts, including RESTful APIs, server-side programming, and database design (SQL and/or NoSQL)
Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
Strong communication skills and ability to effectively articulate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
