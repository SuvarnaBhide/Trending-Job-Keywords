Profile : MERN/PERN Stack Developer

Experience : 1 to 3 years

Location : Balewadi - Pune

Introduction

We are seeking a talented MERN/PERN Stack Developer to become a part of our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will possess robust experience in building scalable web applications using the MERN/PERN stack (MongoDB/PostgreSQL/MySQL, Express.js, React, and Node.js). You will play a critical role in developing and maintaining both backend and frontend components of our web applications.

Responsibilities

 Develop and maintain web applications using the MERN/PERN stack.
 Design and implement RESTful APIs.
 Develop and integrate various third-party APIs.
 Collaborate with team members to ensure high-quality code and software design.
 Ensure the responsiveness of applications.
 Troubleshoot and debug issues as they arise.
 Maintain detailed documentation of application code and features.
 Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
 Stay updated with emerging web development trends and technologies, particularly within the MERN/PERN stack ecosystem.

Requirements

 Demonstrated experience as a MERN/PERN Stack Developer or in a similar role.
 Strong proficiency in JavaScript, MongoDB/PostgreSQL/MySQL, Express.js, React, and Node.js.
 Experience with front-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
 Proven ability working with RESTful APIs and integrating third-party APIs.
 Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies and Git-based version control systems.
 Understanding of DevOps principles and experience with CI/CD tools.
 Excellent problem-solving skills with keen attention to detail.
 Outstanding communication and collaboration capabilities.
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (preferred).
