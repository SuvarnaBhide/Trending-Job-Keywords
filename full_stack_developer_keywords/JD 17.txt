ob description
A Full Stack Engineer with 0-5 years of experience is responsible for developing and maintaining both the front-end and back-end components of a web application
They must be proficient in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js
They must also have a solid understanding of back-end technologies such as Node.js, Python, Java, or serverless architectures using AWS Lambda or Google Cloud Functions
In addition to the traditional technologies, a Full Stack Engineer with 0-5 years of experience may also work with newer and emerging technologies such as GraphQL, REST APIs, microservices, and cloud computing platforms like AWS or GCP
They should have a solid understanding of database systems and be familiar with data storage solutions such as MySQL, MongoDB, or Cassandra
The role requires the ability to design and implement scalable, efficient, and secure web applications and systems
They must be familiar with version control systems like Git and have good problem-solving skills
They should be able to work independently and in a team environment, collaborating with designers, project managers, and other engineers to ensure that the application meets the client's requirements and is delivered on time
Responsibilities
Designing, building, and maintaining the front-end and back-end components of web applications.
Writing clean, maintainable, and scalable code using modern web development technologies and frameworks.
Implementing and integrating APIs, microservices, and other back-end components.
Ensuring the application is responsive, user-friendly, and accessible across different devices and platforms.
Developing and integrating data storage solutions, such as databases and cloud storage services.
Collaborating with designers, project managers, and other team members to gather requirements and ensure project deliverables are met.
Debugging and troubleshooting issues and bugs in the application.
Keeping up-to-date with the latest web development trends, technologies, and best practices.
Participating in code reviews and providing constructive feedback to other team members.
Ensuring the application is secure, with proper access controls, encryption, and other security measures in place.
The responsibilities of a Full Stack Engineer with 0-5 years of experience may vary depending on the size and scope of the project and the needs of the client or company they are working for.

Requirement
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
Strong knowledge of front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js.
Experience with back-end technologies such as Node.js, Python, Java, or serverless architectures using AWS Lambda or Google Cloud Functions.
Familiarity with database systems and data storage solutions, such as MySQL, MongoDB, or Cassandra.
Understanding of RESTful APIs, GraphQL, and microservices architecture.
Familiarity with cloud computing platforms like AWS or GCP.
Experience with version control systems like Git.
Good problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot and debug issues.
Strong written and verbal communication skills.
Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
Attention to detail and ability to deliver high-quality work on time.
